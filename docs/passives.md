Certain passives may be limited by a School, denoted using [square brackets]. These require the character using them to be part of a faction or receive training or special gear before the passive becomes available.
Passives can be used without the necessary school, but cost 1 extra point if the passive costs 1~2, and 2 extra points if they cost 3 or above.
Passives can also be reflavored and renamed at will.
Additionally, passives can freely have their associated status effect changed, as long as the point cost of the status remains the same.

## Uniform
These are passives that can only be gained through story. Nuovo Fabric is especially hard to gain and may involve a quest or mission. The others must be bought or acquired another way.
- (EX) Workshop Uniform I: Lower one resistance by 0.25.
- (EX) Workshop Uniform II: Lower two resistances by 0.25, or one resistance by 0.5.
- (EX) Workshop Uniform III: Lower three resistances by 0.25, or one resistance by 0.5 and another by 0.25.
- (EX) Nuovo Fabric: Same effects as Workshop Uniform III. Additionally, take 2 less Damage and Stagger Damage from attacks.

## Light

- (-2) Skim Prescript [Index]: Start with 4/3 Light in combat.
- (-4) Respiration: Restore 1 extra Light if your Light at the start of the round is 0.
- (-2) Restfulness: Restore 1 extra Light at the end of the round if you didn't act this round.
- (-4) Psych Up: Restore 1 extra Light at the end of the round and gain 1 Strength and Endurance if you didn't act this round.
- (-2) Deep Breaths: At the start of the round, roll a d4. On a 3 or 4, restore 1 extra Light.
- (-2) Insurance Benefits [Life Insurance Office]: Restore 1 extra Light at the end of the round if you were targeted by a one-sided attack this round.
